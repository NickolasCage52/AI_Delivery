# Competitors Review (from live pages)

Below is a quick “screening” of first‑screen structure, CTA placement, trust elements, cases, typography/grid/air, animations, and blockers. Sources: direct page reads from the URLs in `docs/competitors-extract.md`.

## Kenektit Ai — https://kenektit.ru/
- First screen: clear headline about custom AI solutions + primary CTA “Получить консультацию”.
- CTA placement: hero CTA + repeated “получить/связаться” blocks later.
- Trust: stats block (1000+ hours, $100k+ earned, 2 years, integrations).
- Cases: “ИИ отдел продаж” with quantified results (доп прибыль/сэкономлено).
- Typography/grid/air: bold, saturated; short sections; scroll is quick.
- Animations/“wow”: strong (from table); hero is visually loud.
- Friction: few blocks, little process detail, some buttons non‑interactive, AI‑generated visuals reduce trust.

## AiAgency — https://aiagency.ru/

- CTA placement: mostly “Написать” (mailto) + Telegram link for questions.

- Cases: extensive with details in services/casestudy.
- Typography/grid/air: text dense; inconsistent grid across pages.
- Animations/“wow”: video noted in table; not visible in text fetch but likely present.
- Friction: CTAs go to email/Telegram; text overload; less sales‑oriented.

## Последний сайт в таблице — Relevance AI (deep dive)

**Header**
- Clean brand mark, strong primary CTAs (“Try for free”, “Request demo”).
- Navigation supports both product and proof (customers, stories).

**Hero**
- Single, crisp outcome‑driven statement + two CTAs.
- Immediate “try” path reinforces momentum.

**Animations & micro‑details**
- High‑fidelity motion and gamified elements (table notes).
- Apple‑like feel without heavy video; micro‑motion supports clarity.
- “Blur vs clarity” storytelling (table notes) makes value visceral.

**CTA funnel**
- 2‑step funnel: Try free → Demo → deeper resources.
- CTA consistency across sections (features, stories, templates).

**Typography & grid**
- Large, readable headings; short paragraphs; airy layout.
- Proof elements live close to claims (logos + stories).

**Trust**
- Case stories + long logo rows + awards + testimonials.
- FAQ near the bottom for objection handling.

**What to replicate in AI Delivery (no 1:1 copying)**
- Hero with immediate “Try / Get demo” paired CTAs.
- Motion system: small, high‑quality micro‑animations instead of heavy video.
- “Before/after clarity” visual narrative (blur vs focus) to sell outcome.
- Multi‑layer proof stack: logos → stories → testimonials → awards.
- Template/library section framing: “ready‑made solutions” view.
